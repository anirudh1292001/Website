ADMISSION PROCEDURE FOR THE SESSION 2021-22

ADMISSION THROUGH UNIVERSITY COUNSELING

1.Admission to B. Tech first year

85%.of the total intake in B.Tech first year will be filled through Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical university Lucknow Counseling on the basis of JEE main entrance exam rank as per Uttar Pradesh Government order. For further details visit www.aktu.ac.in/upcet2021/

2.Admission to MCA First year

85%.of the total intake in MCA first year will be filled through Dr. AJ Abdul Kalam Technical university Lucknow Counseling as per Uttar Pradesh Government order on the basis of Uttar Pradesh Combined Entrance Test (UPCET) 2021 conducted by NTA. For further details visit www.aktu.ac.in/upcet2021/

3.Admission to MBA First year

85%.of the total intake in MBA first year will be filled through Dr. AJ Abdul Kalam Technical University Lucknow Counseling on the basis of CMAT entrance exam rank as per Uttar Pradesh Government order conducted by NTA. For further details visit www.aktu.ac.in/upcet2021/

4.Admission to B.Tech Second year Lateral entry

100% (10% of total intake in B. Tech first year) B.Tech Second year Lateral entry quota seats will be filled through Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University Lucknow Counseling on the basis of Combined Entrance Test (UPCET) 2021 conducted by NTA as per Uttar Pradesh government order. For further details visit www.aktu.ac.in/upcet2021/

5.Admission under Fee Waiver quota seats

As per AICTE notification Fee Waiver seats for economically weaker section meritorious candidates are also available to take admission under this quota in B.Tech, MBA & MCA courses.
The fee waiver seats will be limited to maximum of 5% of the sanctioned intake in the course and will be over and above of the sanctioned intake.
No tuition fee will be charged from such candidates admitted against such seats. These seats are allotted through UPSEE counseling. For further details visit www.aktu.ac.in/upcet2021

6.Admission To Kashmiri Migrants In First Year

Direct admission to Kashmiri Migrants in first year in each institute is to be made as per the latest instructions of AICTE and orders from U.P. Govt.
Such admissions will be over and above the sanctioned intake and normally limited up to one admission per Institute affiliated to Dr. APJ AKTU, Lucknow.
The detailed information will be published in various newspapers and University website http://www.aktu.ac.in. The candidates, opting for admission under this scheme, need not to appear University Entrance examination. Web portal address is: www.aktu.ac.in/upcet2021/

ADMISSION THROUGH AICTE

1.Direct admission to students of UTs of Jammu and Kashmir and Ladakh (PMSSS) under Prime Minister special scholarship scheme in first year is to be made as per the latest instructions of Department of Higher Education MHRD, Govt of India. Such admissions will be over and above the sanctioned intake. J & K Cell deals with Implementation of Prime Ministers Special Scholarship Scheme to J&K Students to pursue undergraduate studies outside the State of Jammu and Kashmir. The J & K cell is responsible for proper implementation of the scheme to fulfil the objectives of Prime Ministers Special Scholarship Scheme to J&K Students. For further details : www.aicte-jk-scholarship-gov.in/

Admission through Management Quota

1. 15% of the total intake available will be filled by institute as per the laid down norms of Dr. APJ AKTU and State Government.
2. B Tech Course candidate must have passed intermediate examination of U.P. Board or 10+2 level examination or its equivalent with compulsory subjects i.e., Physics and Mathematics and one optional subject as chemistry and obtained at least 60% marks in the above subjects taken together.
3. MBA Course candidate must have passed Bachelor’s degree course of three years minimum duration from any recognized Indian University; or its equivalent, recognized by A.I.U securing minimum 60% marks in aggregate

ADMISSION THROUGH VACANT SEATS COUNSELLING

The vacant seats after counselling of Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Techncial University, Lucknow shall be filled by the Institute as per the laid down norms of Government of Uttar Pradesh and Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow. For B. Tech first year admission candidate must have passed intermediate examination of U.P. Board or 10+2 level examination or its equivalent with compulsory subjects i.e., Physics and Mathematics and one optional subject as chemistry and obtained at least 60% marks in the above subjects taken together. For MBA Course candidate must have passed Bachelor’s degree course of three years minimum duration from any recognized Indian University; or its equivalent, recognized by A.I.U securing minimum 60% marks in aggregate. For MCA Course candidate must have passed Bachelor’s degree course of three years minimum duration from any recognized Indian University; or its equivalent, recognized by A.I.U securing minimum 60% marks in aggregate and for B Tech Lateral Entry vacant seats after counselling shall be filled by Institute as per the laid down norms of Government of Uttar Pradesh and Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow.
